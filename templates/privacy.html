Privacy Policy

Welcome to SmartBot!

SmartBot is an All in One Moderation bot that has a variety of features including moderation, management, giveaways, reaction roles, customized welcome and goodbye messages, server logging, invite logs, leveling and ranks, counting, and much more. This Privacy Policy sets forth our policy with respect to information that is collected from visitors to the Site and users of the App and/or the Services.

Information we collect

When you interact with us through the Services, we may collect information from you, as further described below: Information You Provide: We collect information from you when you voluntarily provide such information, such as when You/Guild admin executes commands on the bot. We store data for giveaways, reaction roles, welcome and leave, logging, and leveling. For welcome, leave, and logging we store the guild IDs and channel IDs. For giveaways, we store the Message IDs as well. For reaction roles, we store the Role IDs. For leveling, we store the user IDs. Message Content: Some guilds may use the server logging feature of the SmartBot. This sends message content to a private logging channel when you delete and edit your message. Users can know if the server logs their messages by typing "/serverinfo". They may opt-out to leave the server if they don't want their messages to be logged if it is enabled.

Data deletion request

Users can get the data removed/deleted on the server through the following procedures: Guild admins can delete the data we store for counting, leveling, welcome, leave, logging, etc. by commands such as "/counting disable" or "/leveling disable". The giveaway data automatically gets deleted as soon as the giveaway ends. They can join our support server to request complete data deletion of the guild.

Website data collection

SmartBot's Website collects information such as: pages you visit in the domain smartbot.co.in. Your IP address, your region and device information. We collect these information for analysis purpose. The documentation page may collect some information, please refer to Gitbook's privacy policy.
